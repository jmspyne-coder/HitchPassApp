Refund Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all paid subscriptions to HitchPass and is part of our Terms of Service.

All sales are final. Subscription fees are non-refundable, except where a refund is required by applicable law. All fees are charged in U.S. dollars (USD).

No refunds for subscription fees

We do not provide refunds or credits for:

  • partial subscription periods or unused time after you cancel;
  • periods during which your account remained open but you did not use the Service;
  • features you chose not to use; or
  • price differences after a plan change or promotion.

The only exception is where a refund is required by the consumer-protection or automatic-renewal laws of your state, province, or country, in which case we will provide the refund the law requires.

Cancel anytime — and keep what you paid for

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, or by emailing supporthitchpass@gmail.com. When you cancel:

  • your subscription will not renew for the next period;
  • you keep full access to paid features until the end of the period you have already paid for; and
  • you will not be charged again unless you resubscribe.

Automatic renewal

Paid plans renew automatically until cancelled. To avoid being charged for the next period, cancel before your renewal date. For annual plans, we send a reminder by email before your plan renews so you have time to decide. See the Terms of Service for full billing details.

Billing problems or disputes

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact us at supporthitchpass@gmail.com before disputing the charge with your bank — we can usually resolve billing issues quickly and directly.
